Pong and Ninky’s Homestay began in 2017 with seven rooms across two floors and expanded in late 2018, adding two more floors managed by Engineer Gener Constantino and Company, resulting in a total capacity of 14 rooms. The company operates as an apartment rental and property management entity, specializing in offering quality accommodations for both short-term stays and long-term leases in Naga City. The properties managed may include individual apartments or entire complexes designed to cater to travelers, students, and professionals seeking residence.

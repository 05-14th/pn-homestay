Accommodations

Bathroom

Both bathrooms in De Leon Apartment (Pong and Ninky’s Home) were clean and well-maintained. Plumbing fixtures, such as sinks, toilets, and showers, were in proper working condition.

Bedroom

The bedrooms were spacious and comfortable. The windows and doors were good in good working order. Closets and storage spaces are well-organized, indicating a high level of care by the tenants. On the first and second floors, there are four units, each containing a single bed slot per room. On the third floor, there are four units, with one double-decker bed per room. Finally, on the fourth floor, there are three units, each with a single bed per room.

Kitchen

The kitchen was modern and equipped with up-to-date appliances. All appliances were functioning properly. There were no leaks or plumbing issues observed.

Living Room

The living room was well-furnished and inviting. There were no visible signs of water damage or mold. The flooring appeared to be in good condition.

Safety and Security

All doors and windows were secure, with functioning locks. Smoke detectors and carbon monoxide alarms are present and in working condition. Each floor is equipped with easily accessible fire, demonstrating a commitment to tenant safety.

Utility

The unitality areas, including the laundry room and any mechanical rooms, were inspected. There were no visible issues with the HVAC system, electrical wiring, or water heater.

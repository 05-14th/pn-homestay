About

De Leon Apartment (Pong and Ninky’s Home) is four-storey building with the land area of 200 sq. meters. It owned by Mrs. Daisy Balane De Leon and managed by Mr. John Arjay G. Balane. The first two floors with, seven rooms for accommodation, were completed in 2017. Subsequently, the decision was made to add two more floors to accommodate more tenants. Construction began again and was completed in late 2018 by Engr. Gener Constantino and Company. Finally, after the construction, it started its role of accommodating tenants who work, study, etc., in the vicinity of Naga City.
